How the drive works

  • Gifts & essentials: toys, winter gear, hygiene kits, and pantry staples.
  • Delivery days: volunteers deliver to families selected by local partners.
  • Every dollar counts: $20 stocks a hygiene kit; $50 fills a pantry box; $100 outfits a child for winter.

Where your gifts go

Funds support gift purchases, food boxes, and last-mile delivery. We partner with local schools, shelters, and community centers to identify families most in need. After the drive, we’ll post a short impact report with photos and totals so you can see the results.
